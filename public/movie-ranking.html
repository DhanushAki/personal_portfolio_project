My Top 3 movies of all-time

  1. Avatar

    2. The masterpiece of James Cameron

    Avatar is its groundbreaking use of technology and visual effects. Directed by James Cameron and released in 2009, Avatar pushed the boundaries of filmmaking and revolutionized the way movies are made.

  2. Terminator 3

    3. Magnum Opus for Arnold Swarschneggar

    Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines is that it marked the return of Arnold Schwarzenegger to the iconic role of the Terminator after a 12-year hiatus. Released in 2003 and directed by Jonathan Mostow, Terminator 3 continued the popular sci-fi action franchise that began with the original Terminator film in 1984.

  3. Titanic

    4. Titanic is that it held the record for the highest-grossing film of all time for over a decade. Released in 1997, the film was directed by James Cameron and starred Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet. It became a global sensation, captivating audiences with its epic love story set against the backdrop of the ill-fated RMS Titanic.
